About

Heritage Amanah International is an independent wealth advisory company licensed by the Financial Services Authority of Indonesia (OJK).

We offer open architecture services and end-to-end financial solutions tailored to the diverse needs of high-net-worth individuals, multi-family offices, retail clients, institutions, and corporations.

Our focus is to help preserve and grow assets for multigenerational families. We combine the strengths and capabilities of the Heritage Amanah Group - with the support of our global networks and independent global fund desks, to deliver the very best service to our clients.

We provide sound advice, customized investment, financial strategies, and concierge-like services. Our services allow us to connect minds without boundaries by providing a platform of extended partners and investment opportunities within the global market.
